Hello! I'm Sara Malvar, an electrical enginner with a master degree and a PhD in engineering. I've started my career at IBM and now I'm a postodoctare fellow at the Research Center for Gas Innovation. I've also worked as a professor for Mechanical and Electrical Enginnering students and I'm now a machine learning mentor, reviewer and instructor at Udacity, Alura and Awari. It's always gratifying to help and teach people as well as partaking in a rabbit hole with students because everyone asks different qestions that would never occur to me.
